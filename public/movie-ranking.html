Wes Anderson Movies to Recommend to Your Friends

"Life Aquatic with Steve Zissou"

Wes Anderson's ability to tell a story is iconic,
and he has tught me a great deal about plot motivation.
This is also my favorite Bill Murray flick!

"The Royal Tenenbaums"

His character development in this one is unsurpassed.
Also, is his use of dialogue to do so.

"The Darjeling Limited"

Anderson's cinimatography is always beautiful, but this film is stunning.
Angelica Huston is the Wild Fem my caged heart needs, always.
